Day One

We arrived by cargo plane and landed on the ice shelf on a bright, frigid, and windy afternoon.

Working at the Bottom of the Earth

I worked in the kitchen washing dishes and cleaning the "galley".

Mcmurdo

McMurdo Station sits on the southern tip of Ross Island and faces the mainland of Antarctica. It is the largest community on the continent and was established in 1956.

Scott Base

When we want to get out of town, we can catch a ride to Scott Base, the New Zealand Base is about a 15 min drive away! (They have a better gift shop)

See the drive from Scott Base to McMurdo!

Wildlife

There are not many animals in Antarctica, but the ones we did see were very cute!

Antarctic Weather

The weather can be very strange in Antarctica. We has storms with winds so strong we could not leave the buildings and snow that piled up in feet over a few hours. The most interestin weather was seen across Ross Sea and on Mount Discovery.

McMurdo is a pretty magical place!
